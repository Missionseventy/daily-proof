Questions

Where is my data stored?

Entirely on your device using your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. Ever. Not even anonymously.

What happens when my trial ends?

You'll see a prompt to upgrade. Your existing session data stays on your device — nothing is deleted. You just can't save new sessions until you upgrade.

How does payment work?

Payments are processed securely via Gumroad. After purchase, you'll receive a confirmation and your access is unlocked immediately.

Can I export my data?

Yes — hit Export in the app at any time to download a JSON file of all your sessions and focuses. Your data is always yours.

No accounts. No tracking. No servers holding your work.
