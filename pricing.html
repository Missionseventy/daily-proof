Questions

Where is my data stored?

Entirely on your device using localStorage. Nothing is sent to any server. Not even anonymously.

What happens when my trial ends?

Your existing data stays on your device. You'll see a prompt to upgrade. Nothing is deleted — ever.

How does payment work?

Payments go through Gumroad — a popup opens so you never leave the app. Access is activated automatically when you return.

Can I export my data?

Yes. Hit Export in the app at any time to download a JSON file of all your sessions. Your data is always yours.
